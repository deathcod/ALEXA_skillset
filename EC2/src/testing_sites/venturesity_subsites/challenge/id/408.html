About Problem Creation Adventure

We at Venturesity want to be the one stop platform for all your professional needs. As such, we are looking to build up our database of competitive programming questions.
If you have the urge to create challenging, and unique competitive programming challenges, all the while working at home in your PJs, then this one's for you.
Calling all those programmers who dream of creating their own competitive programming challenges. All you have to do is to register on our platform. Then create your own challenging competitive programming question along with tags of concepts tested and difficulty level. Also provide us with necessary test cases and a solution for the problem you've created.
Upload these to a bitbucket repository (if you don't have one, create it!) and provide us with the link. Further instructions and guidelines for setting the problems will be made available once you register.
Once you submit the problem and solution, your submission will be evaluated by our team. We will select the best problems and add them to our database. You will be given credit of course. :)
Also, if your problems and solutions are accepted, you will be entitled to compensation on a per question basis. Problems that are difficult to solve will be compensated more than the "easy" ones.
Candidates with the best problems and solutions may even get a chance to work with our amazing team!
So what are you waiting for? Register, get creating and submitting. :)
