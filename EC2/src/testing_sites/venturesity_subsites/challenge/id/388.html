About Building a serverless application at scale (AWS)

We at Accion labs, focus on emerging technology. we are in anything latest and greatest.We are looking for bright young engineers, developer, geeks.Come and meet us at AWS Cloud Security, discuss about what can be built and perhaps become a part of the emerging technology champion team!

Amazon Web Services is one of the world 's most powerful hosting platforms. It can be harnessed in order to create high performance apps, sites and programs. In this LearnUp, you will learn how to build a serverless application at scale using AWS.

However, there are certain prerequisites for attending:

  • Knowledge of Javascript
  • Should have basic AWS knowledge
  • Bring a laptop and charger

    • The duration of the session will be approximately 4 hours.Any longer, and it will be a drag, don 't you think?

    The LearnUp is free and open to all. All you have to do, is to bring your laptop, charger and a mindset for learning!Then you will get the most out of this session.
